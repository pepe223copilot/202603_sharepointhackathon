02. Challenges

Problem3 Pain Points
3 Challenges in Invoice Processing
🗂️

① Invoices Arrive from Everywhere

Email attachments, downloads, and scans arrive through different channels. Inconsistent file names make it hard to know what is where.

👁️

② You Have to Open Every File

To check the payee, amount, or due date, you must open each file. There is no at-a-glance view of status.

✋

③ Everything Is Manual

Even after review, creating bank payment CSVs remains a manual task. There is no automated output.

Core issue: Invoice knowledge is not organized or connected to the next step in the process
